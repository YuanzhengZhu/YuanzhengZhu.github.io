MINI-MANIFESTO


                                      Daniel Eatock


                                     Begin
                                with ideas.
                           Embrace chance.
                       Celebrate coincidence.
                     Ad-lib and make things up.
                 Eliminate  superfluous  elements.
            Subvert expectation. Make something
    difficult look easy.Be first or last.Believe complex
  ideas can produce simple things. Trust the process.
              Allow  concepts  to determine  form.
                Reduce material and production
                   to their essence.Sustain the
                          integrity of an idea.
                           Propose honesty
                              as a solution.
